Usage Guide
Who are the Goons?
Knight, Big Pipe, and Bird's Eye are a roaming Scav squad that spawns on a single map per raid cycle. They're highly dangerous and their location changes each reset.
Reading the tracker
Rows are sorted newest-first. The top row is the most recent player-submitted sighting. All times are relative to your local clock and update every 3 minutes.
Report freshness
Within 30 min — likely current
30 min – 2 hr — possibly stale
Over 2 hr — treat as old data
How to Report
Spotted goons in-game? Use Tarkov Monitor — a background app that you can use to submit your sightings to tarkov.dev while you play. Runs silently alongside the game.
OBS Overlay
Add a Browser Source in OBS and point it at:
/overlay.html
Recommended size: 355 × 104 px. Enable transparent background. Use the PvE / PvP toggle on this page to switch modes — saved automatically and reflected in the overlay.
To force a mode via URL (locks regardless of saved preference):
/overlay.html?mode=regular
